**Abstract**: Given an abstract $n$-polytope $\mathcal{K}$, an abstract $(n+1)$-polytope $\mathcal{P}$ is an extension of $\mathcal{K}$ if all the facets of $\mathcal{P}$ is isomorphic to $\mathcal{K}$. In this paper we explore the problem of building chiral extensions for regular toroids.
