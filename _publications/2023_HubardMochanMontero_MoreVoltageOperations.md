**Abstract**: This is the second volume of a previous paper where the authors intruduce the notion of voltage operations as a combinatorial generalisation of classical operations on convex polytopes. In such manuscript, several examples are shown and some of the basic properties are proved. In this paper we continue with our exploration focusing mainly on the symmetry properties of the objects studied.
