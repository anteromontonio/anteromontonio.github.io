Welcome to my personal/academic website. 
In this website you will fin most of my academic interests/products. 

The [CV](/cv/) tab is a web version of my CV; it basically summarises the contents of this website.
In the [Publications](/publications/) tab you will find a list of my publications.

You can also see a [map](/talkmap/) of where the talks have been given!
<!-- Finally in my [Blog](/year-archive/) you will as many entries as I can write, potentially many of them having little or nothing to to with my academic job. -->

Some words about me: currently, I have a postdoc position at the [Faculty of Mathematics and Physis](https://www.fmf.uni-lj.si/en/) of the [University of Ljubljana](https://www.uni-lj.si/university/) in Ljubljana, Slovenia. 
Most of my research interests are the symmetry properties of combinatorial objects such as polytopes, maps on surfaces, graphs, etc.
In particular, I am interested in construction problems of objects with prescribed combinatorial/symmetry properties.
