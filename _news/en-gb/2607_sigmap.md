This conference is part of the SIGMAP (Symmetries in graphs, maps and polytopes) series and it is the third one that I attend. This conference series live close to my heart: it was the first international conferences that I attended (in 2014, in West Malvern, UK) and the first talk that I gave in English was precisely in this conference. Moreover, this conferences took place in Morelia, my hometown, in 2018. I missed the last edition in Alaska. (2022) due to budget restrictions. 

The website of the conference is here: [SIGMAP 2026](https://leemans.dimitri.web.ulb.be/SIGMAP2026/). In this edition, I am giving a talk "Faithfulness in Maniplexes", which you will eventually find in the [talks](https://anteromontonio.github.io/talks) section of this website.
