title: :champagne: Our paper “Vertex-transitive graphs with small motion and transitive permutation groups with small minimal degree" was published
---

This is joint work with Primož Potočnik. In this paper we offer a classification of vertex-transitive graphs whose motion (smallest number of vertices moved by a non-trivial automorphism) is $4$ or a prime number $p$.

You can see the paper's details [here](http://anteromontonio.github.io/publications/#vertextransitivegraphs) and the paper itself [here](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0097316525000603).
