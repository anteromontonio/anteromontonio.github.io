title: :champagne: Our paper “Chiral extensions of regular toroids” was published!
---

This paper is the result of many years of work alongside with Micael Toledo. In there, we prove that for every n, all but finitely many regular cubical tessellations of the $n$-dimensional torus are facets of a chiral polytope.

You can see the paper's details [here](http://anteromontonio.github.io/publications/#chiralextensionsregular)
and the paper itself [here](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00493-024-00132-0).
