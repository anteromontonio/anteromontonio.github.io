I am giving a talk in the cycle "[Matematični pogovori](https://mathematical-conversations.splet.arnes.si/)" ([Mathematical Conversations](https://mathematical-conversations.splet.arnes.si/)) at the University of Ljubljana on May 20, 2026.  This cycle is organized by the Department of Mathematics and it is intended to bring together young matematicians (postdocs, PhD students). The talk will be about my work on highly symmetric toroids. You can see the announcement of the talk [here](https://www.fmf.uni-lj.si/sl/obvestila/dogodek/2613/antonio-montero-highly-symmetric-tessellations-of-the-n-dimensional-torus/).
