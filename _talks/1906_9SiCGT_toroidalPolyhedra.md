Hotel Kompas, Bled

[Slides]({{ site.url }}/slides/1906_9SiCGT_toroidalPolyhedra.pdf){:target='_blank'}

**Abstract**: In the talk we will review a technique that has been used to classify several kinds of highly symmetric abstract polytopes lying on a toroidal ambient space. We will focus not only on the abstract approach of the classifications but on visualizing such objects.
